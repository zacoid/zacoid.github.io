About Us

Our name says it all. Deluxe Dry Cleaners has been offering the best dry cleaning services in Cypress, Texas and the local Greater Houston area for over 20 years and continues to do so.

Focused on customer service and adaptability, our values have remained the same the day we opened in 1998. With us the day we opened, our employees, and future employees, are always taught to strive for excellence whether it may be a simple shoe repair to cleaning uniforms for specific occassions. Our excellent staff combined with sophiscated technology and machinery removes any ambiguities about how your needs should be treated, ensuring you noticeable quality and care!

Not only does Deluxe Dry Cleaners care about your clothes, but customer satisfaction. Were not pleased with your experience? Don't worry any further. We are happy to redo our services to leave you with a delighted experience. In addition to our customers, one of our few priorities is the environment. We always use clean solvents and environmentally-friendly solutions along with recyclable hangers and bags, catering to your needs with an eye out for the enviornment.
